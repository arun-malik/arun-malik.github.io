Principal Software Engineer · Microsoft Azure Networking

I build AI-powered autonomous operations systems for hyperscale cloud infrastructure. My work focuses on multi-agent architectures that can independently detect, diagnose, and resolve network incidents, shifting operations from reactive human-driven response to proactive, self-healing infrastructure.

Currently at Microsoft Azure Networking, I develop the agentic AI platform that orchestrates autonomous incident resolution across one of the world's largest network infrastructures. My research interests span AI agent design patterns, progressive autonomy frameworks, safety mechanisms for AI-driven infrastructure actions, and the evolution of network operations from rule-based automation to intelligent autonomy.
